Home Privacy Policy

Swastik Amruttulya collects only the information you choose to submit through contact and franchise forms, such as your name, phone number, email address, city, and inquiry message.

Form details may be opened in WhatsApp so you can send your inquiry directly. The website may also store a local copy of submissions and cookie preferences in your browser's local storage for convenience.

We use this information to respond to inquiries, manage franchise interest, improve customer support, and maintain website stability. We do not sell personal information.

You can clear cookies and local storage from your browser settings at any time. For privacy questions, contact us through the details listed on the Contact page.
