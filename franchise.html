FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment required?
The minimum investment for an Swastika Amruttulya franchise starts at ₹50 Thousand- ₹3 Lakhs, which includes equipment, branding, initial stock, and training.
How long does it take to set up my franchise?
From agreement signing to grand opening, the entire setup process typically takes 2–4 weeks depending on the model and location availability.
Is prior food & beverage experience required?
No prior experience is needed! We provide comprehensive training on all aspects of operations, tea preparation, hygiene, and customer service.
What is the expected return on investment?
Most of our franchise partners recover their investment within 4–6 months. Monthly profits range from ₹40,000 to ₹60,000+ depending on location.
Do you provide marketing support?
Yes! We provide complete branding materials, digital marketing support, social media templates, and assist with the grand opening event to maximize footfall from day one.
What is the franchise royalty or revenue sharing model?
We operate on a transparent low royalty model. The exact terms are discussed during the consultation call and outlined clearly in the franchise agreement.
