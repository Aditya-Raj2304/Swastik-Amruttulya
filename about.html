About Swastika Amruttulya

The journey of a humble chai stall that became India's premium tea brand.

A Cup of Tea,
A Legacy of Love

Swastika Amruttulya was born in 2024 from a simple dream — to make every Indian's daily chai a moment of pure bliss. What started as a single tea stall in Jharkhand grew into a movement celebrating India's rich tea heritage.

Our founder, inspired by his grandmother's legendary masala chai, set out to recreate that warmth, aroma, and flavor at scale — without ever compromising on quality. Today, Swastika Amruttulya is a name that thousands of families associate with trust, taste, and tradition.

We source our tea leaves directly from the finest gardens in Assam and Darjeeling. Our spices are hand-selected for potency and freshness. And our recipes? They're treasured secrets passed down through generations.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To serve authentic, hygienic, and premium quality Indian chai to every person — from metro cities to small towns — making quality tea accessible and affordable for everyone.

Our Vision

To become India's most loved and trusted premium tea brand, with 500+ outlets by 2027, creating livelihood opportunities for entrepreneurs while spreading the joy of authentic chai culture nationwide.

Our Journey

From a single tea stall to a growing national brand — here's how we got here.

The First Cup

Swastika Amruttulya opens its first chai stall in Kokar, Jharkhand. The recipe — crafted from a treasured family secret — wins hearts from day one.

2024
2024

Kulhad Chai Initiative

Swastika Amruttulya introduces its signature kulhad chai, promoting traditional clay cup culture and sustainable practices.

First Franchise Launch

Overwhelmed by demand, Swastika Amruttulya launches its franchise model. Within 6 months, 15 outlets open across Jharkhand.
