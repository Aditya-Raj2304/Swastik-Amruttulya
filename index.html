Crafted With Tradition

Born from the heart of India's rich tea culture, Swastika Amruttulya was founded with a single purpose — to bring the authentic taste of traditional chai to every cup. Our master tea makers use time-tested recipes passed down through generations.

Every cup we serve is a promise of purity — premium tea leaves sourced from the finest gardens, fresh spices carefully selected, and milk sourced from trusted local farms.
